# Executable Agent Evaluation

Executable evaluation is better suited than text-only judging for power electronics design agents because engineering validity depends on physical constraints, simulation behavior, and reproducible calculations.

## Red Team

**Steelman against:** Executable checks can be too narrow and may miss creativity, tradeoff quality, or unmodeled engineering constraints.

**How it could be false:** A text judge with expert rubrics may catch conceptual errors that a simple evaluator misses.

**What would change my mind:** Text-only evaluation predicts simulator and expert-review outcomes as well as executable benchmarks.

**Residual doubt:** The best evaluation may combine executable checks with structured expert review.
